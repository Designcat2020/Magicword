JULY 14

You spot me from a far
On the other side of gate
You watch me every step
falling into you
All the smiling in your eyes
"Welcome home"
everything is
perfect in
alternative reality


IN

You are the anchor
You are the boat
You are the storm
And you are the savior
to get me out
of the boat in the storm
and put me in a safe haven
All you and only you
with all of me
Baby
we are in storm of love

FALLING

Into the wonderland
Prison for my mind

That neat white rabbit
"Oh dear, oh dear."
My little prince,
You're nothing but a pack of cards

I choose the blue pill
The story ends

Awakening to the reality
Cold dark lonely

Welcome back to the truth
The promise of God

Rainbow
